What is Explore Hacks and why should I join?

Explore Hacks is a 3-day hackathon that aims to grant both experienced and beginner student programmers the chance to explore the process of ideation to product execution. Participants will gain the opportunity to learn through a variety of workshops and mini-events, allowing them to traverse the different fields of Computer Science. Best of all, Explore Hacks is 100% free!

What will I need to do?

You will need to create a project and submit a 5-minute video that demos your project and pitches the impact its integration within the market of your choice. Upon submission, you must add a URL to your publicly visible code/repository. All submissions will be made through Devpost.

Your project will be judged based on the following criteria:

Feasibility

Impact

Uniqueness

Creativity

You may participate in a team with your friends (1 - 4 people per team).

Please note that the official prompt will be released one day prior to the start of the hackathon.

Please be sure to stay active on our Discord Server, so that you don't miss out on any important announcements!

How are projects judged?
Projects are judged based on 4 criterias: Feasibility, Impact, Uniqueness, and Creativity.
Am I eligible to participate?
Anyone who is a high school student is eligible to participate. The only prerequisite for Explore Hacks is a longing to learn! You do not need to know how to code to join!
Do I need a team to participate?
You may have teams of up to 4 people. You do not need to sign up with a team.
Do I need to pay to register?
Nope! Explore Hacks is completely free.
When and where is the hackathon?
The hackathon takes place from July 23, 2021 - July 25, 2021. The hackathon will be hosted completely online!
